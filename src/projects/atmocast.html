AtmoCast

AtmoCast" is an innovative project that revolves around the visualization of weather data gathered from Internet of Things (IoT) devices. The project's primary objective is to provide users with real-time access to weather information collected by IoT sensors, fostering awareness about local weather conditions and potential impacts.

Key Features

  1. IoT Weather Sensors: AtmoCast is built upon a network of IoT sensors, including the MQ135 and DHT11 sensors, strategically placed to gather precise weather data and pollution levels.
  2. Data Visualization: The project focuses on translating raw weather data into user-friendly visualizations, making it easy for individuals to understand weather patterns, trends, and anomalies.
  3. Impact Awareness: AtmoCast goes beyond data visualization by providing insights into potential impacts and precautionary measures that can be taken in response to specific weather conditions.
  4. IoT Integration: The project highlights your expertise in IoT development, as it involves the integration of sensor data with web-based visualization tools.
